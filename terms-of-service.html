Service Description

PlumberAI provides an AI-powered telephone answering service utilising voice technology from Bland.ai. Our service is designed to intelligently triage and qualify inbound calls for plumbing businesses.

Service Includes:

  • Inbound call reception and triage
  • Lead qualification based on defined criteria
  • Urgency categorisation (urgent/routine)
  • SMS alert dispatch to Client's registered mobile number
  • Automated CRM logging to Client's Google Sheets integration

Service Does NOT Include:

  • Booking or scheduling appointments on behalf of the Client
  • Providing quotations or pricing to callers
  • Guaranteeing minimum lead volume or conversion rates
  • Replacing human staff or live reception services
  • 24/7 availability (subject to Client's configured coverage hours)

Service Level Agreement:

PlumberAI targets 99% uptime on a monthly basis. Scheduled maintenance and third-party outages (Bland.ai, Twilio, Make.com) are excluded from this calculation. We will notify Clients of scheduled maintenance at least 48 hours in advance.
